In the heart of the Kasinga Bend, there was a hippo named Barnaby who had a secret. While the other hippos spent their days lounging in the sun and guarding their territory, Barnaby was obsessed with the stars. Every night, after the safari trucks had left and the river went quiet, he would float on his back, staring at the constellations and dreaming of a world beyond the water’s edge. One evening, he saw a shooting star that seemed to land just beyond the reeds, and he knew it was time for his first real adventure.
